How to Complete

  1. Fill in the freelancer's personal details. Fields marked * are required.
  2. Provide a valid email — it becomes the freelancer's portal login on approval.
  3. Attach a photo and the required documents (passport always; Emirates ID for UAE nationals; residence visa for expats; bank letter).
  4. Use Save Draft anytime to keep partial work — documents can be attached after the first save.
  5. When everything is complete, click Submit for Approval. The request routes to the FL Manager, then FL Admin.

Documents accepted as clear photos (JPG/PNG) or small PDFs. Images are optimised automatically.
